Getting started as a CIViC Curator

CIViC curators are a community of users dedicated to improving the CIViC knowledgebase and its contents.

Prerequisites:

  • Create an account. Process requires login creditials for a supported OAuth provider (Google, ORCiD or GitHub).

Although curators are not required any specific training, background or skill set, curator-generated content can only be "Accepted" once it is reviewed by a CIViC Editor. Editors are experts in cancer genomics, oncologists and other related fields or diseases. This gate-keeper model allows significant contributions from curators with any level of experience while maintaining the quality of an expert-driven resource. Curators can be promoted to Editors once they have demonstrated an aptitude and understanding of the CIViC data model and requirements of cancer variant interpretation.

Whether through streamlining the wording of a variant interpretation, the addition of valid IDs or aliases, identification of key literature sources, small contributions from many curators can make a big impact on how up-to-date and comprehensive the CIViC resource is.
