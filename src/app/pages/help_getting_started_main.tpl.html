Getting started as a CIViC Curator

CIViC curators are a community of users dedicated to improving the CIViC knowledgebase and its contents.

Prerequisites:

  • Create an account. Creating an account requires only that you have login creditials for one of our supported OAuth providers (currently Google, ORCiD, or GitHub).

Although curators are not required to have any specific training, background or skill set, curator-generated content can only be "Accepted" once it is reviewed by a CIViC Editor. Editors are experts in cancer genomics, oncology, pathology and other relevant fields. This gate-keeper model allows significant contributions from curators with any level of experience while maintaining the quality of an expert-driven resource. Curators can be promoted to Editors once they have demonstrated sufficient understanding of the CIViC data model and requirements of cancer variant interpretation.

Even small contributions can make a big impact on how up-to-date and comprehensive the CIViC resource is. Examples of small curation actions include streamlining the wording of a variant interpretation, commenting on existing entries, adding variant IDs or aliases, identification of key literature sources, etc.
