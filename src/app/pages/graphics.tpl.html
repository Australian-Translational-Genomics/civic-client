Curation 'Funnel' Diagram

Illustrates the distillation of clinically actionable knowledge from raw sequencing data, with an emphasis on the bottleneck created by the increasing high-level curation burden on research scientists. Useful to illustrate how CIViC can help alleviate this curation burden by turning the process into a collaborative, social effort. The interpretation bottleneck of personalized medicine by Good et al is licensed under CC BY 4.0
